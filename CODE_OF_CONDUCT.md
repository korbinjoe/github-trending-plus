# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

**Positive:** Respectful language, constructive feedback, empathy, accepting responsibility.

**Unacceptable:** Harassment, trolling, sexualized language, publishing others’ private information, unprofessional conduct.

## Enforcement

Report violations to the repository maintainer via GitHub Security Advisory or private contact. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and ban repeat offenders.
